FAQ
  • What is Cocktail Paradise?

    Cocktail Paradise is a tropical oasis-inspired establishment where you can enjoy delicious cocktail creations that are reminiscent of the lush landscapes, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant culture of paradise. It aims to transport you to a vacation-like experience through its flavorful and exotic drinks.

  • How to use our system?

    1. Visit the Cocktail Paradise website.
    2. Navigate through the different sections using the menu at the top of the page.
    3. In the "Menu" section, browse through the cocktail options available.

  • How to know the details of the cocktail?

    Once you clicked the image of our cocktails, you'll see the ingredients and the alcohol content of each cocktail

  • How to place an order?

    1. To add a cocktail to your cart, click the "Add to cart" button below the desired cocktail.
    2. In the cart, you will see the list of cocktails you have added, along with the total price.
    3. To proceed with your purchase, click the "Buy Now" button in the cart.
    4. Fill in your delivery details, including your name, email, phone number, and message (if applicable).
    5. Submit the order by clicking the submit button.
    6. If you have any further questions or need assistance, you can go to the "FAQs" section to find frequently asked questions and their answers

  • How long do cocktails in Cocktail Paradise last?

    Our cocktails are freshly made thus it should be consumed within a few hours to ensure the best taste and quality.

  • How do we ensure that the cocktails arrive safely?

    To ensure the safe delivery of cocktails, we take special care in packaging and handling. Our team uses secure and durable packaging materials that are designed to protect the drinks during transit.
